The city government page

The City of Bloomington is governed by Mayor Mark Kruzan who was elected in November 2003. This page contains links to information about Bloomington city government including boards, commissions, City departments, City Council and legislation. If you have difficulty finding the information you need, please e-mail info@bloomington.in.gov for further assistance.
